About: Who I Am

This is the section that prompted me to write this whole page. When I started my job at NeoGenomics I got a homework assignment. I was supposed to write a short bio for the company newsletter. After starting, I discovered how hard it was for me to write about myself. I even tried doing it in the third person to remove myself from the picture. After a small amount of effort I gave up and asked Gabi my wife to write it for me. She did and excellent job of it, and inspired me to write my own about me.

Her good work made me realize it had been a while since I took a good look at myself in the mirror. I should not be afraid to talk about myself, and so this site is an attempt to remedy that problem.
